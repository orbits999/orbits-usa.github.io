AI Product QA — 4 Years

Dental AI Insurance Platform

FDA-Cleared ML Platform · Healthcare Insurance Technology

Served as the primary QA engineer responsible for validating an FDA-cleared dental AI platform's insurance product — a system that analyzes dental X-rays using computer vision and machine learning to support claims adjudication decisions for major US insurance carriers.

Core work involved verifying that AI model outputs — caries detection percentages, radiographic bone level measurements, and calculus identification — were correctly processed and applied against each payer's specific medical necessity logic trees to produce accurate accept, pend, or deny adjudication outcomes. Also performed rigorous DB and UI cross-validation to ensure data integrity between the AI processing layer and reviewer-facing interfaces.

About Orbits

Quality Engineering
at the Frontier

We are a US-based technology consulting firm bringing senior QA expertise and engineering capability to healthcare, insurtech, and regulated SaaS companies.

Orbits was founded on a simple belief: that quality engineering in complex, regulated industries requires genuine domain expertise — not just test scripts. When you're validating AI models that inform clinical decisions, or testing federal health exchange infrastructure, generic QA simply isn't enough.

Our lead consultant brings over a decade of healthcare technology QA experience spanning AI-powered dental insurance platforms, federal CMS Marketplace programs, COVID response applications, and enterprise SaaS products. We combine rigorous manual test strategy with modern automation and AI-assisted tooling to deliver thorough, traceable coverage efficiently.

Backed by an experienced software engineering team, Orbits can scale from individual QA consulting engagements to full development projects — giving clients a single trusted partner for quality and technology.
